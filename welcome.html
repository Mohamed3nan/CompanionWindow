Welcome to Companion Window!

Thank you for installing Companion Window! This extension allows you to transform any webpage into a floating, always-on-top window for seamless multitasking.

One Quick Step

To get started, you need to reload your tabs once. Click the button below to reload all tabs:

How to use Companion Window:

  1. Click the extension icon in your toolbar, or
  2. Right-click anywhere on a webpage and select "Open in Companion Window", or
  3. Press Alt + C to open a tab in Companion Window
