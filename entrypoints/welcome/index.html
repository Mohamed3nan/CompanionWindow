Welcome to Companion Window

To activate the extension in your current browser session, you need to refresh your open tabs. This is a one-time setup step.

Getting Started 🚀

Companion Window lets you transform any webpage into a floating, always-on-top window for seamless multitasking. Here's how to use it:

  • Pin and click on the extension icon to open the Companion Window
  • You can also right-click on any webpage and click "Open in Companion Window"
  • Use the keyboard shortcut to quickly open the current page in Companion Window

Features 🌟

  • Always-on-top floating window
  • Context menu integration
  • Minimize to a compact view
  • Customizable window size
  • Optional Keyboard shortcuts for faster workflow
  • Optional Menu button in the top-center corner for additional options(Minimize, Reload)

Tips & Tricks 💡

Tip: Resize the window by dragging its edges to fit your needs.
Tip: When minimized, click anywhere on the window to restore it.
Tip: Use the three-dot menu in the top-center corner for additional options(Minimize, Reload).
